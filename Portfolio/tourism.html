Flew & Brew

At the Center for Advanced Research and Technology, I investigated various tourism websites and how different companies created a brand image to market their luxury vacations. I continued my research alongside two other partners as we studied Hong Kong, Italy, and Singapore.

Finally, we accumulated enough information regarding the various cultures and their traditions to create a website that could market our product: native coffee experiences. As each country has its connection to food, we sought to capture that connection with coffee. By selling each of these experiences to consumers, we eventually immersed ourselves in the corporate and development aspects of product design.

One of the core aspects of completing this project was to create the website for our fantasy business entitled Flew & Brew. We utilized HTML and CSS for this project, and as my CSS skill increased, I even began incorporating media queries for mobile, tablet, and desktop versions.

After completing the project, I can reflect, a pivotal CART Habit of Mind, on the effort I exerted while creating the website. Ultimately, I knew that I would need to alter how I would make my navigation tools and drop-down menus in the future to allow for a better user experience.
